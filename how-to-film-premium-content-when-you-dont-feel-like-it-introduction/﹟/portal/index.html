How to Film Premium Content When You Don't Feel Like It

Creating premium content can sometimes be a struggle, especially during the colder, darker months of the year. Seasonal affective disorder, the lack of motivation, and the general dreariness of winter can make it tough to produce engaging content for your subscribers. In this article, we'll explore tips and tricks to help you push through those moments when you don't want to film premium content.

Loosen Up with a Drink

Sometimes, having a drink can help you relax and get in the right mindset for creating content. It's important not to overdo it, as you still need to be able to work your equipment and communicate clearly. However, a drink or two can help you loosen up and feel more comfortable in front of the camera.

Turn on a Sensual Playlist

Music can set the mood for creating content. Create a playlist of soft, sexy music that helps you get into the right headspace. Remember to pause the music when filming videos to avoid copyright issues, but having it on while taking photos can help create a sensual ambiance.

Pretend You're Sending Photos to Someone Else

Imagine that you're taking photos for someone special, like a partner or a situationship, rather than for your premium content platform. This can help you feel more motivated and engaged in the process. However, it's essential to maintain a clear boundary between your personal and professional life to avoid issues in your relationships.

Collaborate with Others

Working with a partner, friend, or another content creator can make the process more fun and engaging. Having someone else to bounce ideas off of and laugh with can make it easier to create content, especially when you're feeling unmotivated.

Watch Adult Content

While controversial, watching adult content can sometimes help you get in the mood to create your own. Choose the type of content you consume wisely, and use it as a quick source of inspiration when you're feeling stuck.

Use Snapchat Filters

If you're struggling with the idea of getting ready and putting on makeup, using Snapchat filters can help you create content without having to spend hours on your appearance. Be careful not to overuse filters, as some subscribers might prefer seeing your natural beauty.

Film Everyday Activities

Consider filming content while doing everyday tasks like laundry, showering, or working out. This can help you create content without feeling like you need to put on an elaborate show.

Film Content Around Other Events

If you already have makeup on for a date or event, take advantage of your appearance and film content the same day. This can help you minimize the amount of time spent getting ready solely for content creation.

Film a Little Every Day

Instead of batching content creation, try filming a small amount every day. This can help you maintain variety in your content and ease the daily workload.

Give Yourself a Break

Finally, if you're truly not in the mood to create content, it's okay to take a break. Sometimes, pushing yourself to create content when you're not feeling it can lead to subpar results. Give yourself permission to take a day off and try again tomorrow.

Conclusion

Creating premium content can be challenging, especially during difficult times or when you're feeling unmotivated. However, by using the tips and tricks outlined above, you can push through those moments and continue producing engaging content for your subscribers. Remember, it's essential to find the methods that work best for you and adapt them to your unique situation. By doing so, you can continue to grow your platform and build a successful business.

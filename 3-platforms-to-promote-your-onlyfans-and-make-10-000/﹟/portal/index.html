3 Platforms to Promote Your OnlyFans and Make $10,000

Platform 1: Instagram

Creating a Link Tree

To promote your OnlyFans on Instagram, you will need to create a Link Tree. Link Tree is a service that allows you to create a custom list of links to share on your Instagram bio. Since you can't directly link your OnlyFans account on Instagram, Link Tree provides a workaround. Sign up for a free account on the Link Tree website and add your OnlyFans link. Then, paste the Link Tree URL in your Instagram bio.

Using Reels to Increase Visibility

The goal on Instagram is to create a reel that gets a lot of views and attracts people to your page. Once they visit your profile, they'll see your Link Tree and, ultimately, your OnlyFans account. To increase your chances of creating a viral reel, research popular and trending topics, and try to post at least one reel per day. The more content you produce, the higher the likelihood of gaining more visibility and attracting more subscribers to your OnlyFans.

Platform 2: TikTok

Linking to Your Instagram

On TikTok, you'll want to direct people to your Instagram account to access your OnlyFans link. In your TikTok bio, write something like, "The link you want is on my Instagram [Cat Emoji or Hot Dog Emoji]." This will signal to viewers that there is something interesting on your Instagram page, prompting them to visit your profile and potentially subscribe to your OnlyFans account.

Creating Content for TikTok

To increase your chances of success on TikTok, try to post at least one video per day, or even more if possible. Just like with Instagram, the more content you put out, the more likely you are to gain visibility and attract subscribers. Additionally, you can repurpose content between Instagram and TikTok by saving your videos to your camera roll and posting them on both platforms.

Platform 3: Reddit

Setting Up Your Reddit Profile

Reddit can be a great place to start promoting your OnlyFans, especially if you want to remain anonymous. To begin, create a Reddit account with the same username you use on your other platforms, if possible. In your Reddit profile, include a bio and a direct link to your OnlyFans account.

Finding Subreddits to Post In

Find 5 to 10 subreddits that fit your aesthetic and target audience. These can be based on physical features, interests, or any other relevant category. Post in these subreddits daily, using a small selection of photos that showcase your content without giving everything away for free. Remember to always wear clothes, lingerie, or swimwear in your photos to entice potential subscribers.

Growing Your Presence on Reddit

When starting out on Reddit, it's essential not to overwhelm the subreddits you're posting in. For the first week, only post in a couple of subreddits and gradually add more each week. This will help you avoid getting your account temporarily blocked for spamming.
